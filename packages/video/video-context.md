# MarketingAlphaScan — Progression Video Context

## What This Is

A 2:53 motivational progression video showing the 12-year journey of a marketer learning to code and finally shipping a production application in 2026. Made with Remotion (React-based video framework). Format: 1920×1080 landscape.

## Who Made It

Ian Carlos — a marketer from Puerto Rico with 10+ years of MarTech experience across top organizations. Currently between roles, dedicated full-time since November 2025 to building his portfolio. This app is the culmination of that work.

## The App: MarketingAlphaScan

A MarTech intelligence platform that forensically audits any website's marketing technology stack. Not a SaaS startup — a portfolio piece showcasing product management, design, and engineering skills.

**Tech stack:** Next.js 15, Fastify 5, BullMQ, Redis, Patchright (Playwright fork), Supabase, Stripe, Google Gemini AI, Recharts, Framer Motion, Tailwind CSS, Turborepo, Docker, deployed on Vercel + DigitalOcean.

**Key stats:** 42 intelligence modules, 5 forensic scan phases, AI-powered synthesis, headless browser automation with stealth fingerprinting.
