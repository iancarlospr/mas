# What is MarketingIQ?

MarketingIQ is not another website health score. It's a **forensic assessment of your marketing technology stack** — measuring how well your tools are configured, whether your tracking is accurate, and if your compliance posture holds up under scrutiny.

## The 8 Weighted Categories

MarketingIQ is computed across 8 categories, each weighted by its impact on marketing effectiveness:

| Category | Weight | What It Measures |
|----------|--------|------------------|
| Analytics Integrity | 20% | Tracking accuracy, consent compliance, data quality |
| Paid Media Attribution | 18% | PPC tracking, conversion pixels, landing page setup |
| Performance & UX | 15% | Core Web Vitals, mobile responsiveness, carbon footprint |
| Compliance & Security | 15% | DNS security, legal pages, consent management |
| Martech Efficiency | 12% | Tag governance, tool overlap, stack complexity |
| SEO & Content | 10% | Metadata, structured data, content optimization |
| Market Position | 6% | Traffic trends, competitive positioning |
| Digital Presence | 4% | PR, careers, investor relations, support infrastructure |

## How Scores Work

Each module within a category produces **checkpoints** — specific verifiable findings with a health status:

- **Excellent** (100%): Best-in-class implementation
- **Good** (75%): Solid but room for improvement
- **Warning** (35%): Issues that need attention
- **Critical** (0%): Broken or missing functionality
