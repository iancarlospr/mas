## 📊 What Gets Scanned

### 🔐 Security & Compliance
Email authentication (SPF, DMARC, DKIM), SSL certificate quality, security headers, HSTS preload readiness, cookie consent compliance, GDPR/CCPA audit, pre-consent tracking detection, subdomain exposure via certificate transparency, and industry-specific regulatory checks for banking, healthcare, and finance.

### 📈 Analytics & Measurement
GA4 configuration validation, measurement ID verification, consent mode integration, data layer structure analysis, cross-domain tracking, tag manager audit (duplicate TMS detection, piggyback tags, server-side tagging), ad pixel fire verification across Meta/Google/TikTok/LinkedIn/Twitter, click ID preservation, and behavioral tracking detection.

### ⚡ Performance & Experience
Core Web Vitals (LCP, CLS), page weight breakdown, render-blocking resource detection, image optimization (modern formats, lazy loading), font loading strategy, third-party script impact, carbon footprint estimation, mobile responsiveness at 375px viewport, touch target sizing, accessibility overlay detection, WCAG 2.1 audit (alt text, ARIA landmarks, heading hierarchy, form labels, focus indicators), console error capture, and error monitoring tool detection.

### 🔍 SEO & Content
Title/meta description quality, Open Graph and Twitter Card validation, structured data (JSON-LD) analysis, robots.txt and sitemap validation, canonical URL verification, hreflang implementation, keyword portfolio analysis, ranking stability (gaining vs. losing keywords), content freshness, and AI discoverability (llms.txt).

### 💰 Paid Media
Active ad detection across Facebook Ad Library and Google Ads Transparency, ad creative analysis with AI, paid landing page audit (tracking parity, conversion readiness, page focus), paid keyword portfolio, CPC efficiency, estimated monthly spend, and competitive overlap on paid keywords.

### 🧩 MarTech & Infrastructure
CMS/CDN/framework/hosting detection via fingerprint matching, marketing technology stack mapping across 18 categories (100+ tools), stack coherence analysis, form builder detection, ecommerce platform identification, payment processor detection, pricing/trial page detection, authentication provider detection, and product-led growth signal analysis.

### 🏢 Brand & Digital Presence
Press/newsroom page quality, media contact availability, careers page and ATS provider detection, investor relations portal audit, customer support infrastructure (help center provider, status page, community forum, support channels), news sentiment analysis, social media sentiment, Google review velocity and trends, and Google Business Profile completeness.

### 🌍 Market Intelligence
Estimated monthly traffic volume, geographic traffic distribution, organic keyword portfolio, SERP position distribution, top referring domains, domain authority and backlink quality, brand search demand across 12 keyword variations, Google Shopping presence, and competitive landscape mapping.

## 🖥️ Tech

Built as a monorepo with a Next.js frontend and a dedicated scan engine backend. The scan engine runs a real stealth browser — not a headless scraper that gets blocked by every CDN. It navigates like a real user, handles bot protection automatically, and collects data that HTTP-only tools simply can't see.

Every module failure is isolated. Nothing cascades. The scan always completes with whatever intelligence it could gather.

The AI synthesis sends the full extracted data (not summaries) to the model with detailed assessment rubrics and industry benchmarks. For ad creative analysis, actual screenshots are sent as images. This isn't a wrapper around a checklist — it's a strategic analyst reading your entire marketing infrastructure.
